Brendan Griffiths

Brendan Griffiths is a graphic designer, programmer, and educator based in New York City. He is a partner in the design practice ZUT ALORS!, and currently serves as Assistant Professor of Interaction Design at Parsons School of Design. He holds a BFA in Multimedia Design from the University of Oregon and an MFA in Graphic Design from Yale University.
